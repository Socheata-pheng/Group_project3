By adopting from shelters and rescue organizations, you not only provide a loving home to an animal in need but also help reduce the number of homeless pets

A caring home needed

When considering adoption, it's essential to select a pet that matches your lifestyle and preferences.
Additionally, consider whether you want a dog, cat, or another type of pet.
Our Pawties are in need of a caring home and a responsible owner.
Pawties can sometimes be stray, abandoned, abused by their last owners.
We are looking for a dedicated and responsible owner to help look after them.
We are always in need of people who are willing to foster a cat or dog until they could find a permanent home.
If you are interested in being a foster, feel free to contact us and let us know.
