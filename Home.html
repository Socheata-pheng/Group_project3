Welcome to Paws

As well as being a volunteer organisation, Phnom Penh Animal Welfare Society is also a full-service Veterinary Clinic,
and this forms an integral part of our organisation and the volunteer work we perform to improve Animal welfare in Cambodia.
The fees we receive from our Veterinary work are one of the main sources of finance that we use to fund the many life-changing
projects we undertake not just for the benefit of the Animals, but also for the Cambodian people.

By using our Veterinary Practice you are directly supporting PPAWS improve the well being of the many
abandoned and stray cats and dogs specifically in Phnom Penh but also, in other parts of Cambodia where we have
partnerships and work to support those Animal rescue and Animal welfare organisations too.
